About Drop Hostel

Your Perfect Escape in Kandy

The Drop Hostel is located in the beautiful hills of Kandy next to the Dharmaraja College. It has an amazing view above the lake, mountains and city of Kandy with stunning sunsets from the balcony. The fan-cooled rooms have beautiful mountain and garden views. They include a wardrobe and an en suite bathroom with shower facilities and towels.

Drop Hostel is located in Kandy, 900 metres from Temple of the Tooth (Sri Dalada Maligawa). Featuring a restaurant, it also offers complimentary bicycle rentals and free WiFi access in its public areas.

Rooms & Wood Cabbin

Double Rooms

Welcome to our cozy Double Rooms – perfect for couples or solo travelers seeking a private space. Each room is tastefully decorated with modern furnishings and features a comfortable double bed, an en-suite bathroom, and a balcony with a beautiful view. Enjoy complimentary Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and a peaceful atmosphere that ensures a relaxing stay.

Dorm Rooms

Our Dorm Rooms offer a vibrant, social experience for travelers on a budget. Equipped with comfortable bunk beds, individual lockers for your belongings, and shared bathrooms, these rooms are ideal for meeting fellow adventurers. With plenty of space and modern amenities like free Wi-Fi, you’ll have everything you need for a comfortable and affordable stay.

Wood Cabbin

Indulge in the ultimate comfort with our spacious Wood Cabbin, designed for families or groups of friends looking for a luxurious yet homey stay. Featuring multiple bedrooms, a private kitchen, a large living area, and an outdoor terrace, the villa offers privacy and all the conveniences of home. Perfect for extended stays or group getaways, this retreat is nestled in a peaceful part of the hostel, ensuring both relaxation and easy access to all the excitement nearby.
